About Us

We are a non-governmental organisation committed to creating positive and lasting change in society. Our mission is to uplift communities by focusing on sustainability, child welfare, and dignity for every individual.

Cause & Impact

Mission 'Green'

We planted and nurtured 1000 saplings to promote a greener environment. By involving local residents and volunteers, we create awareness about environmental responsibility and work towards a greener, healthier planet for future generations.

One Meal

We provided nutritious meals to 200+ underprivileged children to fight hunger and malnutrition. Proper nutrition helps children grow healthy, stay in school, and dream of a better tomorrow.

Sarvam Vastra

We distributed clean and wearable clothes to people in need, restoring dignity and comfort. This initiative took aiming no one is left exposed to harsh weather or social neglect.
